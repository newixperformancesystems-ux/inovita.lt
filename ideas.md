# Inovita Landing Page – Dizaino Idėjos

## Idėja A – „Surgical Precision" (p=0.07)
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets dark SaaS
**Core Principles:** Grid-based asymmetry, typographic hierarchy, data-forward, no decorative noise
**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0C0C0F) base, electric amber (#F5A623) as single accent – communicates urgency and money
**Layout Paradigm:** Left-heavy asymmetric grid; hero text bleeds to left edge, stats float right
**Signature Elements:** Thin horizontal rules as section dividers; monospaced numbers for stats; sharp rectangular cards with left-border accent
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover reveals data; cards expand inline; no popups
**Animation:** Numbers count up on scroll; section entries slide from left; no bounce
**Typography:** Space Grotesk (headings, bold) + Inter (body) – clinical precision

---

## Idėja B – „Dark Forge" (p=0.08)
**Design Movement:** Industrial brutalism meets premium SaaS
**Core Principles:** Raw power, heavy typography, stark contrast, zero fluff
**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#0A0A0E) background, electric lime-green (#A8FF3E) accent – aggressive, growth-oriented, money
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed sections with diagonal cuts; hero takes 100vh with oversized headline; stats section uses 3-column masonry
**Signature Elements:** Diagonal section dividers (clip-path); glowing green CTA buttons; bold stat numbers with thin unit labels
**Interaction Philosophy:** Every scroll triggers a micro-reveal; CTA buttons pulse subtly
**Animation:** Staggered card entrances; diagonal wipe transitions; number counters
**Typography:** Syne (display, extra-bold) + DM Sans (body) – brutalist energy

---

## Idėja C – „Obsidian Edge" (p=0.06)
**Design Movement:** Luxury tech minimalism
**Core Principles:** Restraint, precision, premium feel, every element earns its place
**Color Philosophy:** True black (#080810) + warm white text + single accent: deep teal (#00C9A7) – calm authority, trust, growth
**Layout Paradigm:** Centered hero with generous padding; problem cards in horizontal scroll on mobile, 3-col on desktop; stats in full-width band
**Signature Elements:** Frosted glass cards (backdrop-blur); subtle grid overlay on hero; teal glow on hover
**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, deliberate; nothing jarring; premium feel
**Animation:** Fade-up on scroll; glass cards shimmer on hover; progress bar in quiz
**Typography:** Clash Display (headings) + Satoshi (body) – luxury tech

---

## Pasirinkta: Idėja B – „Dark Forge"
Agresyvus, rezultatais orientuotas dizainas su electric lime-green akcentu. Syne + DM Sans šriftai. Diagonal section cuts. Pulsing CTA. Tai atitinka „aggressive, results-driven" toną iš prompto.
